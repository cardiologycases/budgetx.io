Welcome to BudgetX Support

Designed to assist you in managing your finances effectively, BudgetX acts as your personal income-expense calculator, providing comprehensive features to track and analyze your financial activities. Whether you want to keep a close eye on your monthly income, monitor your expenses, calculate loans, determine the time needed to purchase a product, or even stay updated on exchange rates, BudgetX has you covered.

Key Features:

  • Managing Monthly Income
  • Efficient Expense Tracking
  • Total Income and Expense Overview
  • Loan Calculator
  • Time to Purchase Calculator
  • Exchange Rate Tracker

BudgetX is a comprehensive and user-friendly Flutter app that simplifies your financial management. By offering advanced features such as income tracking, expense categorization, total income and expense overviews, loan calculations, time to purchase estimations, and exchange rate tracking, BudgetX empowers you to take control of your finances.

Managing Monthly Income:

BudgetX offers a seamless solution for tracking your monthly income. By simply inputting your salary or any additional income sources, the app enables you to organize and categorize your earnings. With a user-friendly interface, you can easily add income values and view them on a monthly basis. This feature gives you a clear picture of your financial inflow and helps you plan your budget more effectively.

Efficient Expense Tracking:

Monitoring expenses is equally important as managing income. BudgetX provides an intuitive expense tracking system that allows you to categorize your expenses by type. Whether it's groceries, rent, utilities, entertainment, or any other expenditure, you can input the details and assign them to the respective categories. The app then generates detailed reports displaying your expenses on a monthly basis, giving you valuable insights into your spending patterns.

Total Income and Expense Overview:

To give you a holistic view of your financial situation, BudgetX includes a summary page that displays your total income and expenses. This overview provides you with a comprehensive understanding of your financial health and enables you to identify areas where you may need to adjust your spending habits. By visually presenting your income and expenses side by side, BudgetX empowers you to make informed decisions about your financial goals and aspirations.

Loan Calculator:

BudgetX goes beyond simple income-expense tracking and offers a dedicated page to calculate loans. Whether you're planning to take out a mortgage, a personal loan, or any other form of credit, this feature helps you estimate monthly payments, interest rates, and the overall cost of the loan. By entering the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term, BudgetX instantly generates an accurate breakdown of your repayment schedule, allowing you to plan your finances accordingly.

Time to Purchase Calculator:

For those looking to save for a specific purchase, BudgetX includes a convenient tool to calculate how much time it will take to accumulate the necessary funds. By inputting the product's price and your desired savings amount, the app calculates the time required based on your income and expenses. This feature helps you set realistic financial goals and provides a clear timeline for achieving them, empowering you to make well-informed purchase decisions.

Exchange Rate Tracker:

BudgetX recognizes the global nature of personal finances and includes an exchange page that keeps you updated on currency exchange rates. This feature enables you to easily convert between different currencies, ensuring you have accurate information when dealing with international transactions or travel expenses. With real-time exchange rate data, BudgetX helps you make sound financial decisions when dealing with foreign currencies.

Conclusion:

BudgetX is a comprehensive and user-friendly Flutter app that simplifies your financial management. By offering advanced features such as income tracking, expense categorization, total income and expense overviews, loan calculations, time to purchase estimations, and exchange rate tracking, BudgetX empowers you to take control of your finances. With this app by your side, you can make informed decisions, achieve your financial goals, and ultimately achieve financial peace of mind.
